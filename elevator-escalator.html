Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our elevator and escalator maintenance services

How often should elevators be maintained?

Elevators should undergo preventive maintenance monthly, with comprehensive inspections every 3-6 months depending on usage and building regulations.

What is included in emergency elevator service?

Our 24/7 emergency service includes rapid response, safety assessments, immediate repairs, and passenger rescue if needed.

Do you provide escalator maintenance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive escalator maintenance including step replacement, handrail servicing, and safety compliance checks.

Are your technicians certified?

All our technicians are certified and licensed professionals with extensive training in elevator and escalator maintenance and safety standards.
