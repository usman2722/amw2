Our Story

AMW, established in 2008 and headquartered in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, is a trusted provider of Integrated Facilities Management and Technical Services.

Over the years, we've grown into a respected organization known for operational excellence, innovation, and delivering tailored solutions across commercial, residential, industrial, and public sector projects.

We offer a full spectrum of services—from routine maintenance to complete facilities management—designed to enhance the performance and value of our clients' assets.
